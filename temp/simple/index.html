8 Journal Prompts for Deep Self-Discovery

Self-discovery is a journey, not a destination. These prompts are designed to help you peel back the layers and understand yourself better.

  • 1.

    What is a behavior or thought pattern you have today that no longer serves the person you are becoming?

  • 2.

    In which area of your life have you been playing small? Describe what 'thinking big' would look like.

  • 3.

    If you were completely unafraid of failure, what is the first change you would make?

  • 4.

    What are three values that are non-negotiable for you? How are you living them today?

  • 5.

    When do you feel most like yourself? What are you doing in those moments?
